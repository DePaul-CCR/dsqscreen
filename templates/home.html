Welcome to the free online screener for Myalgic Encephalomyelitis/Chronic Fatigue Syndrome (ME/CFS)


The online screener is based on the DePaul Symptom Questionnaire (DSQ), a validated instrument for assessing ME/CFS Symptom burden. This screener utilizes a portion of the DSQ's items, and asks between 1 and 17 questions to determine a predicted diagnosis. For more information, follow one of the links below.
