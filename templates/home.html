This online screener is based on the DePaul Symptom Questionnaire (DSQ), a validated instrument for assessing ME/CFS Symptom burden.

The online screener asks 5 questions. It compares your answers with domain scores from over 3,000 responses, and provides you the proportion of ME/CFS participants with similar scores. After the screener, you have the option of answering additional questions for more detailed results. A layout of each step is shown below. For more information, follow one of the links above.


Disclaimer: This screener is not meant to provide medical diagnoses. Please consult with your physician for diagnostic concerns.
