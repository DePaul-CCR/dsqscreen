Please read the following consent form carefully

If you agree to store your data for this screener, it will involve completing a 5-question survey. Each question will assess your symptom burden.

The questions can be completed online or from you mobile phone or personal computer. You must complete each question before moving on to the next one.

Are there any risks involve din participating in this study?

You may be upset or uncomfortable answering some questions, but you may stop at any time if you do not want to continue. There is the possibility that others may find out your answers, but we have put protections in place, such as encrypting your personal information before storing it, giving it as much protection as a password.

Are there any benefits to participating in this study?

You may not directly benefit from this study. However, you will be able to return and track your data over time. Your participation may contribute to researchers better understanding ME/CFS.
